1. Equator Monument

The Equator Monument or Equator Monument is on Jalan Khatulistiwa, North Pontianak, West Kalimantan Province. The location is about 3 km from the center of Pontianak, towards the city of Mempawah.
This monument becomes one of the icons of Pontianak City tourism and is always visited by the public, especially tourists who come to Pontianak.


Information :
  • Name : Equator Monument or Khatulistiwa Park
  • Address : Jl. Khatulistiwa, Batu Layang, Siantan, Kota Pontianak, Kalimantan Barat
  • Opening Hours : 07.30 – 21.00 WIB

2. Digulist Monument

Although it is more popular with the Equator Monument, Pontianak also has other monuments that are not less interesting to visit such as the Digulis Monument for example. The shape is similar to pointy bamboo making it also often known as the Sharp Bamboo Monument. This monument was built to commemorate the services of the youth of West Kalimantan, especially the city of Pontianak in seizing independence.


The location itself is located on Jl. A. Yani precisely at the Untan roundabout (Tanjungpura University) made it so interesting that it could be an instagramable photo spot.
  • Name : Digulist Monument or Tugu Digulis
  • Address : Jl. Ahmad Yani, Bansir Laut, Pontianak Tenggara, Pontianak, Kaimantan Barat
